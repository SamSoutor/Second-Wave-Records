About Us

Second Wave Records first opened in June of 1986 and had a mission to carry out for all potential customers; to provide for them the best selection of what the music industry has to offer. To help get this message across, it was decided that different genres should be provided in order for people to see what appeals to them the most.
In the 30 years of operation, we have had many different people come in to tell us what they are looking for and believe it or not, we even expanded their tastes in the world of music.
Our store is open between the hours of 9:00am-10:00pm and is open 7 days a week, with the exception of certain holidays.

Events

Remember Bob Marley? We here at Second Wave Records like to pay tribute to him every year on May 11th. Marley made a huge impact on the reggae genre and we often like to show our customers what it meant to music fans worldwide. During this event, we play his music 24/7 and showcase various memorabilia. We've often had some customers become new fans because of this. Want to show your support for the Bob Marley Tribute? Feel free to stop by during store hours when you have time.
Ah the early 90s! A time when things were looking very bright after the 80s wrapped up. One of the best things to come out of that decade was Nirvana. As the pioneers of grunge music, this band helped paved the way for an alternative sound heard in bands such as Pearl Jam, Soundgarden and Stone Temple Pilots. We here at Second Wave Records show our appreciation every April 5th by celebrating the band's impact. Want to show your support for Nirvana Appreciation Day? Feel free to stop by during store hours when you have time.
RapFest is probably our most ambitious event. At this event, which is held every August 8th, we showcase the most famous figures in the world of rap music and what they did to pioneer the genre. Since there are so many artists to highlight, we've often had multiple fanbases giving us feedback on how to make the event bigger than before. What really defines this event is the rap battles. At the event, you can challenge others with your skills and see if you can become the champion. Want to show your support for RapFest? Feel free to stop by during store hours when you have time.
